Philatourism

Did you know that during 2020, stamp collecting saw a resurgence?

Given the inability to travel or to socialize normally, it makes sense that people confined indoors would pick up new or rediscover old hobbies. But why stamp collecting? For some, it was dusting off an old collection. For others it was a way to introduce children to a new hobby. Whatever the reason, membership in stamp clubs started to grow again and stamp prices at auctions saw a measurable increase.

Whatever the reason, when travel is impossible, stamp collecting is another way to "visit" new countries. This site shows examples of the beauty and culture of countries around the world.
